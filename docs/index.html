IronHenge shows a new way to look at Tower Defence genre. The world suffers from a disaster caused by excessive sun activity. The only way of survival is going under ground to find water. Hostile life forms forced humans to find a way to defend themselves. Rotating rings were built around the caves to protect their residents. Give people hope in this sun torched hell, play the role of defence system administrator and spin it in your favour!

Download

Ironhenge presents a brand new take on towers defences. Protect the entrance to survivals cave from enemies coming for far and wide. Bulid towers on the ring and rotate it to keep all the roads safe.

As the game moves on, new types of both enemies and defences are presented. You have to divide your attention among all different entrances through the ring.

You also want to make sure that you let yours allies in before the enemies have a chance to attack them.
